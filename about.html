About Us

24 Capital is a NY based, rapidly evolving Direct Funder in the FinTech Space.

We assist small and moderate sized businesses, and quickly obtain working capital for their various needs. 24 capital has been an industry leader in providing alternatives to traditional funding sources, and an innovator in providing cash advances and other financial services to businesses in the retail, restaurant and services industries. 24 Capital can provide funds for merchants in as fast as 24 hours.

We pride ourselves on exceptional customer service, speedy responses, and outstanding offers & rates beating out the competition.
